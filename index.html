ABOUT US

"Handa Ka Na Ba?" is an Interactive Map designed to empower the residents of Barangay Marulas, Valenzuela City, in their preparedness for flooding. This initiative recognizes the anxiety and uncertainty that floods can bring to the community. By developing an interactive map, the project aims to provide vital information on flood risks, evacuation routes, and local emergency resources, making it easier for residents to stay informed and safe.
